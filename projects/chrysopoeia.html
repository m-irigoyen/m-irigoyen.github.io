Chrysopoeia

Summary

Date 2017 - December (one week)
Tech Custom engine : C++ / SFML

Bind atoms together to form a target molecule in this Opus Magnum inspired puzzle game.

  • Features all the functionality of the piston arms of the original game : pushing, pulling, rotating the arm or the molecules

Context

Made during a two day game jam, the original idea was to make a "multiplayer Opus Magnum" puzzle game. The multiplayer part was dropped due to lack of time.
The design and programming was entirely done by me.

I couldn't get it past the prototype stage during the jam.
