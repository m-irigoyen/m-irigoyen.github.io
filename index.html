Bio

I got my first computer in grade school along with a pile of game CDs. Almost twenty years later, a reliable way to keep me off the screen has yet to be found.
At least I'm not hyperactive anymore !

I love a good story and as a child games looked like an amazing storytelling medium. I liked science as well, so going into computers was the logical option. While studying for a Software Engineering degree I never stopped playing and making games, including them in my student life through creating game development clubs and organizing game jams.

My first job at Allegorithmic is where I got to build software used by all the studios I knew and loved. This leaves me wanting to take my talents into actual game production, which I guess is why you're reading this !
